About Boikanya

Boikanya is a modern, clinically driven healthcare company dedicated to transforming the lives of individuals living with metabolic and chronic conditions through the power of movement. Rooted in compassion, science, and measurable outcomes, Boikanya exists to bridge the gap between traditional healthcare and sustainable lifestyle change.

Biokinetics care grounded in professional ethics

Boikanya is a female-owned biokinetics practice providing movement-based assessment and rehabilitation services. The practice is led by a University of the Free State (B.Biokinetics) graduate and operates within the professional scope and ethical standards of the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA).

The practice focuses on supporting individuals seeking to improve physical function, manage movement-related conditions, or maintain long-term mobility through structured exercise and education.
