Frequently Asked Questions

General

  • What is Biokinetics? Biokinetics is a healthcare profession focused on using exercise and movement to improve health, function, and quality of life, especially for those with chronic conditions or injuries.
  • Where is the practice located? Eco-Park Virgin Active, 270 Witch-Hazel Avenue, Centurion, Gauteng, 0157.
  • How do I book an appointment? Use our online booking system powered by Bookem to find an available slot and book directly.
  • What are your business hours? Monday–Thursday: 7:00am–18:00pm, Friday: 7:00am–15:00pm.
  • How do I contact the practice? Email admin@boikanya.com or call (+27) 067 172 8818.

Appointments & Bookings

  • Can I reschedule or cancel? Yes, but please provide at least 24 hours' notice to avoid a cancellation fee.
  • Is a referral required? No referral is needed. However, if you are under medical supervision, we can coordinate with your healthcare provider.
  • Are sessions covered by medical aid? Many medical aids cover biokinetics services. Please check with your provider and request a statement after your session.
  • What should I bring? Comfortable clothing, a list of medications, and any relevant medical reports or imaging.

Services

  • What conditions do you treat? Orthopaedic injuries, neurological conditions, chronic diseases, pre- and post-natal care, and more.
  • Do you offer group classes? Yes, group movement classes are available. See our Services page for details.
  • Do you offer home visits? Home visits may be arranged on request, subject to availability.

Other

  • Is parking available? Yes, parking is available at Eco-Park Virgin Active.
  • Is the practice child-friendly? Yes, we offer play-based movement sessions for children.
  • How do I get updates? Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter for the latest news and tips.
